Instructions

Try to generate a Phyllotaxis on the left to match the Phyllotaxis on the right. Change the angle the dots are generated at, the size the dots start at, the spacing between dots, the color of the dots, and whether the dots increase in size as more are generated. Click the "Restart Phyllotaxis Generation" button to update your phyllotaxis, and click the "Compare Phyllotaxis" button to see if they are the same. If all the values match up, your correct guess counter goes up by 1 and a new phyllotaxis to match is generated. If the values don't match up, then your incorrect guess counter increases, and you will have to try again.
